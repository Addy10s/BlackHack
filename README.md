## Welcome to BlackHack, the blackjack-based dungeon crawler!
## To Play -> http://github.com/Addy10s/BlackHack/releases
## Gameplay
In the map, move around with WASD, walking through open doors to close them, and if you walk on any red-bordered tile, it's an encounter, whether an upgrade shop, a trash can, a battle, or the boss.
### In standard Encounters:
Click *Hit* to draw a card, but try not to overshot your limit. If you beat the goal, you can win the round( by clicking *Hold*), but if you push your luck to get exactly your limit, it makes the game easier later on.
###  Shop
It shows Upgrades and Gold Cards, upgrades can give you extra lives, or increase your limit, and Gold Cards can be flipped over to ignore the value of the card. You buy by pressing the price
### Trash
You can sell cards you don't want, each card costing 10 Coins, to optimize your deck.
### Boss Encounter
The boss acts as a player, who draws cards from their own deck whenever you have more cards than them. You win if you don't overshoot your limit, and the boss does.
